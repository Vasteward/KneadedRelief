About the Practitioner

I find it important to educate my clients with the wonderful benefits of massage therapy. The key to an exceptional quality of life is to preserve our health.


As we get older in age, our body wears down more and more. It is essential to practice routine maintenance to decrease the chances of chronic health conditions that could compromise our well-being. As a massage therapist, I am able to inform and apply techniques to motivate my clients to take control of their health and vitality.
